Discover Sagada

Sagada is a unique destination that offers a blend of adventure, culture, and natural beauty, making it a must-visit for travelers looking to experience the charm of the Philippine highlands.

Sagada Details

Things to Do
  • Visit the famous Hanging Coffins of Sagada, a traditional burial practice of the indigenous Igorot people.
  • Discover Bokong Falls, a beautiful waterfall located a short hike from the town center.
  • Explore Sagada Weaving shops to see traditional techniques and purchase handmade products.
How to Get There

If you prefer to drive, take the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) and then the Mountain Province roads to reach Sagada.
