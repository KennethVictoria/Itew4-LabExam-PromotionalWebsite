Discover Manila

Manila, the capital city of the Philippines, is a bustling metropolis rich in history, culture, and modern attractions. It serves as the political, economic, and cultural center of the country, offering a wide range of activities for visitors.

Manila Details

Things to Do
  • Explore the historic walled city of Intramuros, featuring Spanish colonial architecture and landmarks like Fort Santiago.
  • Visit Rizal Park (Luneta Park), an iconic park dedicated to the national hero, Dr. Jose Rizal.
  • Discover Binondo, the oldest Chinatown in the world, known for its vibrant food scene and cultural heritage.
How to Get There

Ninoy Aquino International Airport (NAIA) is the main airport serving Manila, with numerous international and domestic flights. From the airport, you can take a taxi, ride-hailing service, or airport shuttle to your destination in the city.
