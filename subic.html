Experience Subic Bay

Subic Bay, known for its beautiful beaches, adventure parks, and rich history, offers a variety of activities for visitors.

Subic Bay Details

Things to Do
  • Visit Ocean Adventure, a marine park with dolphin and sea lion shows and interactive marine life experiences.
  • Explore Zoobic Safari, a wildlife park where you can see exotic animals and enjoy feeding sessions.
  • Relax at All Hands Beach, a family-friendly beach offering water sports and various facilities.
How to Get There

Getting to Subic Bay:
Subic Bay is accessible by bus or private vehicle from Manila. Several bus lines operate daily trips to Olongapo City, which is adjacent to Subic Bay. The travel time is approximately 2 to 3 hours depending on traffic.

*Common modes of transportation within Subic Bay and Olongapo City include taxis, jeepneys, and private vehicles.
